# Trialsplained

**Clinical trials, explained like a human.**

Trialsplained is a plain-language clinical trial finder for patients, families, carers, and anyone who has ever tried to read a ClinicalTrials.gov listing and given up.

Built on International Women's Day 2026 as part of SheBuilds — a 24-hour build challenge powered by Lovable.

🌐 [trialsplained.lovable.app](https://trialsplained.lovable.app)
🗓 Built: 8 March 2026

---

## The Problem

Clinical trial consent forms are written at undergraduate reading level. Most adults in the UK read at a 14-year-old level (Lancet, 2024). Over 80% of clinical trials face recruitment delays — not because people don't want to participate, but because the language locks them out.

ClinicalTrials.gov lists over 400,000 studies. Almost none of them are written for the people they need.

Trialsplained is the plain-language layer that has been missing.

---

## What Trialsplained Does

- **Searches** publicly registered clinical trials from ClinicalTrials.gov and the NIHR Clinical Research Network
- **Rewrites** complex trial descriptions into plain English using AI
- **Displays** honest, clear trial cards — including phase, commitment, placebo arms, and side effect acknowledgements
- **Enables** sharing via WhatsApp and email
- **Allows** printing a GP-ready trial summary
- **Links** every summary directly back to the official registry listing

What it does NOT do: rank trials, recommend trials, collect data, or suggest that any trial is right for any individual.

---

## Who It's For

- Patients recently diagnosed with a condition
- Family members and carers researching options
- Members of the public curious about longevity, mental health, or preventative medicine
- Anyone who has ever been baffled by medical jargon

---

## Features

- Plain English AI summaries with last-verified dates
- Patient view and Professional view toggle on every card
- Phase I risk callouts — honest about early-stage uncertainty
- WhatsApp share and email share
- Print-to-GP summary
- Session-based saved trials (no account required)
- Accessibility: text size controls (A / A+), high-contrast mode, screen reader support
- No sign-up, no email capture, no data collection
- Disclaimer on every page
